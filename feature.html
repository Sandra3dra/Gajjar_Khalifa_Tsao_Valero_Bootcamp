The company is used as a subtle way of introducing and promoting what the company serves. Smoothies are made by blending fruits; fruits are not only healthy but rejuvenate and adds to the fresh feeling. Hence, the name “Healthy Blends”. Name itself is enough for the branding and clearly asks people to adopt healthy blended drinks and avoid caffeine and unwanted sugars which are harmful to the body. We produced Healthy Blends brand values and a way to encourage and build a healthy community. Colors inspired by nature are used in the brand and other branding mediums to attract health-conscious community and to build the strength of the community in the future.
